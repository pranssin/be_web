About US

What the project achieved in technical terms.
LETS TALK NUMBERS!

As more and more enterprises require heavy voice services as a part of customer support and feedback, they need a system which is cost effective. This is a cloud based communication system for voice services. These voice services will be supported by VoIP for calling over the cloud. SIP is used to exchange caller id information. An effort to reduce the en-to- end delay has been made and also to increase the mobility of the system. A software based PBX is used and the cloud-based voice services are enabled using Raspberry Pi. Asterisk platform together with the Raspberry Pi will form the cloud middleware. Call routing will be made efficient using IVR(Interactive Voice Response).

This work is concerned with establishing a small-scale telephony system such that the clients on the incoming don't have to wait for long and getting rid of the traditional hardware used.

Cloud Telephony is simply telecom applications delivered under the ‘cloud’ model. With this model, SMBs have access to the same cloud benefits as large enterprises. A cloud telephony system allows business professionals to depend completely on the progressive calling services and focus more on the core business activities. This amazing release of communication solutions that work over Internet has given leverage to employees to work anytime anywhere. Greater flexibility provided by cloud telephony services is the best reason to invest in them.

Features

Why to choose our cloud telephony solution?
Flexibility

In a cloud based communication system, the professionals are allowed to increase and reduce the numbers of telephone systems depending upon the current business requirements.

High End Service Features

Some of the popular services include interactive voice response, push SMS, voicemail, call forwarding, automated call distribution, etc.

24/7 Maintenance and Support

Everything is handled by the third party service provider.

No Money Needed

Our product uses end devices and cloud solution to establish the telephony system which reduces the initial capital expenditure greatly.

No Physical Infrastructure Needed

Our solution removes the use of conventional PBX systems with cloud solutions providing higher quality audio sounds and regular analysis.

Awesome Interactive Voice Response(IVR)

The IVR system is easily customizable with various language support and human voices.
